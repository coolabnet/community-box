# About Community Box

Community Box is a research project and practical toolkit designed to help communities establish their own digital infrastructure. We provide comprehensive guides, hardware recommendations, and software configurations to empower local communities to build and maintain their own community networks.

## Our Mission

This project aims to lower the technical barriers for communities seeking to establish their own digital infrastructure. Our approach combines:

- **Research**: Analysis of previous real-world implementations of local community infrastructures
- **Documentation**: Current research on hardware and software platforms
- **Testing**: Extensive testing on both physical devices and cloud servers
- **Guides**: Practical step-by-step guides for deployment and maintenance

## Project Origins

This project was sponsored by **APC's Locnet project** and developed by **Luandro**.

The Association for Progressive Communications (APC) is an international network of organisations that was founded in 1990 to provide communication services for peace, human rights, development and protection of the environment. The Locnet project specifically focuses on supporting local community networks through research, documentation, and capacity building.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Hardware Questionnaire**: An interactive tool to help you choose the right hardware for your community's needs
- **Quickstart Guides**: Step-by-step guides for various hardware platforms and software configurations
- **Research Papers**: In-depth analysis of community network implementations
- **Service Directory**: A global directory of community networks
- **Solar Calculator**: Tools for planning sustainable, off-grid deployments

## Get Involved

Community networks thrive on collaboration. Whether you're a technical expert, a community organizer, or just curious about decentralized digital infrastructure, there's a place for you in this project.
